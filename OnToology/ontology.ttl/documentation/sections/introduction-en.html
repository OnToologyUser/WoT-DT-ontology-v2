Introduction back to ToC

The convergence of Digital Twins and the Web of Things faces significant interoperability challenges due to a lack of data model standardization. The WoT-DT Ontology addresses this gap by offering a shared, formal vocabulary to describe a Digital Twin's complete lifecycle. Its modular architecture and alignment with key standards, such as the W3C WoT Thing Description and SAREF, facilitate the creation of discoverable, scalable, and interoperable digital ecosystems.
